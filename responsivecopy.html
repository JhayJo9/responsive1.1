Gun Park (박종건 Park Jong Gun) is a major character in Lookism. He is a fighter of legendary status and the personal bodyguard of the HNH Chairman's daughter, Crystal Choi. He is also one of the "10 Geniuses" raised by Charles Choi and one of the three children trained by Tom Lee.


Gun has shown consistently to be one of the strongest characters in the series. He has a mixed fighting style with complete mastery of Kyokushin karate, high proficiency in Aikido, and Capoeira as shown when Olly identified one of Johan Seong's copied moves from Gun,[10] his fight with Goo Kim[11] and his Brazilian kick.[12] He is also shown to be proficient enough in weapons to fight using tonfas[13] and to teach Eli Jang how to use stun batons.
